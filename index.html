About us

Over the years, we have built our reputation on excellence in shipping and logistics, with a steadfast focus on serving our clients and addressing all of their needs. Our hard work, consistency, and desire to excel in everything we do have led to the growth of M & H Transport Ltd. M & H Transport Ltd had a modest beginning in 2018 with a few trucks. Today, we have one of the most recent fleets of power units, dry vans, and refrigerated trailers on the market. From the outset, our goal has been to assist and support our business partners in obtaining reliable, dependable, and high-quality service in transporting goods from shippers to recipients. The principles of our business have remained the same: fast and safe transportation of goods without compromising on customer service, while adhering to all safety and government regulations as per DOT and NSC. When we handle your loads, one thing you will notice is that we have some of the most experienced certified drivers. Your loads are in the hands of people who are experts and experienced in the logistics world. Commitment and excellent customer service are the keys to the success of our business. Reach out to us today and experience our service firsthand. To meet your current and future transportation needs, we have one of the most recent fleets of vehicles, trained drivers, and friendly staff who will provide you with the best customer care in the logistics world.
